Integrate ReadReplicaRouter into the replica request pipeline with health/lag updates.

- Add `read_replica_router: ReadReplicaRouter` (plus `replica_health` array) to Replica state; initialize in `Replica.init` with leader id, replica list, and `metrics.Registry.clusterMetrics()`

- Update router health/lag on existing heartbeat/view-change paths (e.g., when receiving ping/pong, mark replica healthy; when replication lag metrics update, call `updateReplicationLag`)
- Ensure failover to leader when no healthy replicas (router returns `.leader`)

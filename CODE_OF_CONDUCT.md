# Code of Conduct

ArcherDB is an open-source project and expects respectful, professional
collaboration in issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other
community spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss technical disagreements in good faith.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and decisions rather than people.
- Help keep discussions on topic and actionable.
- Respect the time and boundaries of maintainers and contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or hateful language.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance on project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or moderate comments, issues, pull requests, or
other interactions that violate this policy. Severe or repeated violations may
lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security or
maintainer contact channel rather than escalating publicly. Use the guidance in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) when private reporting is needed.
